Greek Islands

In June 2020 I will be travelling for 3 weeks to various greek islands such as Ios, Santorini, Mainland Greece, Mykonos. I have been working part time since August in order to pay for this holiday with complete independence. We are planning to depart from Dublin on the 17th of june and return home on the 8th of July. We plan to stay in a mixture of apartments and pensions on the islands, mostly located near the beaches or town centres of Greece. Unfortunately one of the most costs that will be incurred will be on travel as, besides the usual airline costs,we will also have to pay hefty ferry cost to get from island to island as many of the islands do not have airports or only have very small airports.

Kolkata, India

In February 2018, I participated in a HOPE Foundation immersion program on the ground in Kolkata, India In order to be able to take part in this I had to raise over €2400 fo the Hope Foundation in advance of this trip. The overall trip, including travel lasted 10 days due to the long flights and stopovers in Dubai. While in Calcutta we spent pur time visiting various orphanages and group homes for children and also two hospitals set up by the Hope Foundation for street and slum children and their parents. While over there I learned valuable lessons and information about the problems we face regarding global inequity and child neglect. I also learned of the jonourable work the HOPE Foundation does in Kolkata and would hope to return there one day to see the orphanages and group homes once more and also togive back to this worthy charity once more.
