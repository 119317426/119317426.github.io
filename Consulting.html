Hyperautomation

Named as one of the top 10 strategic Technology trends for 2020, Automation is the use of technology to perform work that ordinarily requires human input.Hyperautomation, however, enables companies to automate more complex work and defined as the application of advanced technologies including AI and machine learning to increasingly automate processes and augment humans.Simply, hyperautomation is the mixture of existing automatic technologies to expand human capabilities.Hyperautomation differs from regular automation in that it takes an ecosystem of technologically advanced tools and combines them to create a new way to work. the benefits of hyperautomation are numerous with hyper-automation creating a workplace that is always informed, agile and able to use data and insights for quick and accurate decision-making.Hyper-automation can also provide your business and its leaders with benefits such as; improved employee satisfaction, advanced analytics, an educated workforce and improve employees capacity.

Multiexperience

Multi-experience development platforms (MXDPs) help enterprises to rapidly scale their applications across a range of devices and platforms, it is widely regarded as the future of app development. MXDPs can help you develop a single application that can be deployed on a variety of devices. This greatly shortens down your efforts and gives brands more control over their digital experiences.Multi-experience offers complete user experience which is particularly useful as it is no longer enough for buyers in the market to simply interact with a mobile app or web application. Multi-experience offers a multitude of benefits, namely the reduction time to market for mobile apps, the internal operations efficiencies, the improved control over actual deployment and the elimination of potential security risks. The biggest barrier in building a multi-experience development platform is aligning the IT and the business experience, however Consulting Co. can offer expert advice on how to overcome this issue.

Distributed Cloud

Distributed cloud is the application of cloud computing technologies to interconnect data and applications served from multiple geographic locations.In an IT sense, distributed means hat something is shared among multiple systems which may also be in different locations. Distributed cloud speeds communications for global services and enables more responsive communications for specific regions.Cloud providers use the distributed model to enable lower latency and provide better performance for cloud services.Examples of current and emerging applications that benefit from a Distributed Cloud Infrastructure include: content delivery networks (CDNs), data storage with regulatory compliance, hybrid cloud platforms, IoT data processing, video processing, VR/AR, machine learning, and control/decision systems
